Lasagna

Description

This is a homemade recipe that is easy to make in addition to being quite filling!

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Noodles
  • Onion
  • Tomatoes
  • Sugar
  • Spices
  • Egg
  • Cheese

Step-by-step process

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
