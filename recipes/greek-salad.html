Greek Salad

Description

This is a traditional homemade Greek salad that is quick to make while being delicious

Ingredients

  • Lettuce
  • Sliced cucumbers
  • Large tomatoes
  • Black olives
  • Green bell pepper
  • Red bell pepper
  • Feta cheese
  • Onion
  • Olive oil
  • Lemon
  • Black pepper

Step-by-step process

  1. Combine romaine, cucumber, tomatoes, olives, bell peppers, and red onion.
  2. Whisk olive oil, lemon juice, oregano, and black pepper together in a small bowl.
  3. Pour dressing over salad, toss well to combine, and serve.
